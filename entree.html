Creamy Lemon Chicken Piccata

What is this recipe?

This Italian-American chicken dish that's known for its light and zesty sauce. This recipe is considered to be resturant-worthy despite only taking 30 minutes! This recipe serves up to 4 people!

INGREDIENTS TIME!

  • 1lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt & Ground Black Pepper
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (31g)
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoons water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth (237g)
  • 2 lemons
  • 3/4 cup heavy whipping cream (170g)
  • 1/4 capers (30g)
  • Hot cooked pasta or mashed potatoes to serve with

How to actually make it

  • Slice the chicken breasts in half horizontally so that you end up having 4 thin chicken breasts. Season them with salt and pepper!
  • Beat an egg into a shallow bowl with 1 tablespoon of water. Add flour to another bowl and the breadcrumbs to a third bowl. Coat the chicken on both sides in the flour, then egg wash, and then breadcrumbs.
  • Heat 2 tablespoons butter and 2 tablespoons of oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add the chicken and cook for about 3 minutes on each side, or until cooked through. Set aside to the side.
  • Reduce heat to a medium low. Add chicken broth, cream juice from the lemons and capers. Season with salt and pepper if needed. Allow the sauce to cook and bubble for 3-5 minutes. Then place the chicken back to the pan.
  • You can serve this with any type of side you want. Preferably with mashed potatoes or hot cooked pasta.

A refreshing meal for you to enjoy!
