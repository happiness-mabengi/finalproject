Prosciutto, Pear & Burrata Salad

What is this recipe?

It is a light and slightly sweet-savory salad that is commonly served as an appetizer or even as refreshing main dish. It combines burrata, prosciutto, ripe pears, and fresh greens that often finished off with a simple vinaigrette. This recipe can serve up 6 people. And it takes 10 minutes to make it!

INGREDIENTS TIME!

  1. 8 cups baby arugula
  2. 1/3 cup olive oil
  3. 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  4. 6 thin slices prosciutto, torn into strips
  5. 3 small red pears, sliced
  6. 8 ounces burrata cheese, cut into 1-inch pieces
  7. 3 tablespoons capers, drained
  8. Coarsely ground pepper

How to actually make it

Place arugula in a large bowl. Drizzle oil and vinegar. Transfer this to 1 large or 6 individual serving bowls. Top with prosciutto, pears, burrata and capers. Sprinkle pepper. If needed, you can add more oil and vinegar.
